Zoe has years of experience in almost every department, including directing, music directing, stage managing, and running tech (QLab who?).

She wrote her first musical soon after she began composing, a one-act adaptation of The Witches by Roald Dahl, performed in 2017 with the permission of the Dahl Foundation. Since then, she's written several musicals and standalone songs, and graduated from Berklee with a minor in writing for musical theatre. Most recently she's been working as the bookwriter/lyricist on Monsters, Boosicals, And You along with composer Perri Webb, as well as working on lyrics for Stephen Glantz' stage adaptation of Joseph Green's 1936 film Yidl With His Fidl.

Zoe also attends as many live productions as she can! From professional to high school theatre, you can find her in the audience, asking cast members for their autographs.
